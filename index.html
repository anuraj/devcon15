Shiju Varghese

His areas of interest include Cloud Solutions Architecture, Application Containers, Distributed Systems, RESTful Microservices Architecture, Enterprise APIs and Mobile Backend as a Service. You can follow him on twitter at https://twitter.com/shijucv.

Yanesh Tyagi

Yanesh Tyagi, Delivery Manager with Millennium Consultants, has extensive 19 years of experience working on different technologies, mostly in Microsoft space. He has been working on .NET platform since it's early days. He specializes in developing enterprise level solution architecture. Currently he is focused on delivery excellence by implementing continuous delivery. Yanesh has worked in various domains including BFSI, Travel and eCommerce. Yanesh is also involved in couple of open source projects.

Anuraj Parameswaran
